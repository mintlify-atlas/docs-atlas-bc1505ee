DNSHE is a non-profit platform built to lower the barrier for developers, students, and open-source enthusiasts to establish a digital presence. Keeping the service free for everyone depends on every user acting responsibly. Misuse harms not only DNSHE's infrastructure but all the legitimate projects that rely on it.

## Permitted use

DNSHE subdomains may be used for any lawful purpose, including personal websites, development environments, open-source projects, automated testing pipelines, SaaS applications, and SSL certificate automation.

## Prohibited activities

The following are strictly banned on all DNSHE domains:

- **Phishing and fraud** — impersonating organisations, stealing credentials, or running deceptive schemes
- **Malware distribution** — hosting, linking to, or serving malicious software of any kind
- **Illegal content** — any content that violates applicable law in the jurisdiction of operation or the jurisdiction of DNSHE's infrastructure
- **Spam and abuse** — mass unsolicited messaging or harvesting of user data without consent

<Warning>
DNSHE enforces a zero-tolerance policy against illegal and abusive activity. Any domain found in violation will be **immediately terminated** without prior notice, and the associated account may be permanently suspended.
</Warning>

## Reporting abuse

If you discover a DNSHE subdomain being used for phishing, malware, fraud, or any other abusive purpose, report it immediately through one of the following channels:

- **Abuse report form:** [https://www.dnshe.com/domainabuse/](https://www.dnshe.com/domainabuse/)
- **Email:** [abuse@dnshe.com](mailto:abuse@dnshe.com)

<Note>
For urgent security incidents — such as active phishing campaigns or malware distribution — email [abuse@dnshe.com](mailto:abuse@dnshe.com) directly. This channel is monitored for time-sensitive reports.
</Note>

Reports are reviewed by the DNSHE team and acted upon as quickly as possible. Providing specific domain names, URLs, and a brief description of the violation helps expedite the review process.
