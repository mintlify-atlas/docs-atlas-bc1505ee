## Why DNSHE?

DNSHE makes it easy to get a real domain for your side project, CI environment, or personal site — completely free. Unlike services that lock you into tight renewal windows, DNSHE gives you a full 180-day window so your services stay online even if you miss a notification.

## Get started in four steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    Sign up at [dnshe.com](https://www.dnshe.com) — no credit card required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Claim your subdomain">
    Search for your preferred prefix and choose a suffix (e.g., `myapp.us.ci`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add DNS records">
    Point your subdomain to your server by adding an A, AAAA, or CNAME record. Changes propagate globally in seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Keep it alive">
    Renew from the dashboard or automate renewals via the REST API within your 180-day window.
  </Step>
</Steps>
